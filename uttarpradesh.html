🏛️ Top Tourist Attractions in Uttar Pradesh

🌍 UNESCO World Heritage Sites

🕌 Taj Mahal, Agra
One of the Seven Wonders of the World, this ivory-white marble mausoleum is a symbol of eternal love and India's most iconic monument.
UNESCO Site Architecture Photography Must-Visit
🏰 Agra Fort
A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this red sandstone fortress served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for generations.
UNESCO Site Historical Mughal Architecture
🏛️ Fatehpur Sikri
A perfectly preserved Mughal city built by Emperor Akbar, showcasing the best of Indo-Islamic architecture.
UNESCO Site Ghost City Akbar Architecture

🕌 Sacred Religious Sites

🕉️ Varanasi (Kashi Vishwanath)
One of the oldest living cities in the world, Varanasi is the spiritual capital of India with sacred ghats along the Ganges.
Sacred Ganges Ancient City Spiritual
🙏 Mathura & Vrindavan
The birthplace of Lord Krishna, these twin holy cities are filled with temples and attract millions of pilgrims.
Krishna Pilgrimage Temples Festival
🏛️ Ayodhya
The legendary birthplace of Lord Rama, recently home to the magnificent new Ram Mandir temple complex.
Ram Mandir Epic New Temple Pilgrimage
🕌 Allahabad (Prayagraj)
The sacred confluence of Ganges, Yamuna, and mythical Saraswati rivers. Hosts the world's largest religious gathering - Kumbh Mela.
Triveni Sangam Kumbh Mela Three Rivers Sacred Bath

🏰 Historic Cities & Monuments

🏛️ Lucknow
The city of Nawabs, famous for its refined culture, Awadhi cuisine, and magnificent monuments like Bara Imambara and Rumi Darwaza.
Nawabi Cuisine Culture Architecture
🦌 Khajuraho
UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its group of Hindu and Jain temples with intricate erotic sculptures.
UNESCO Site Temples Sculpture Medieval
🦏 Dudhwa National Park
A biodiversity hotspot protecting tigers, elephants, rhinoceros, and over 400 bird species in the Terai region.
Wildlife Tigers Safari Nature
🏰 Jhansi
Historic city known for the valor of Rani Lakshmibai, featuring the magnificent Jhansi Fort and rich freedom struggle history.
Rani Lakshmibai Freedom Fighter Fort History
🌊 Chitrakoot
A serene town associated with Lord Rama's exile, featuring beautiful waterfalls, caves, and temples amidst natural beauty.
Ramayana Waterfalls Nature Peaceful
🏛️ Sarnath
Where Buddha delivered his first sermon after enlightenment. Features ancient stupas, museums, and peaceful Buddhist monasteries.
Buddha Buddhism Ancient Peaceful
